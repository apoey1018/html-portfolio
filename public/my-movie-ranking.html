Adrian's Best Movies

Here are my favorite 3 movies I had ever watched

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

This is the movie that introduced me to the magical world of the Harry Potter series. It is a well-adapted movie that sets the background for the rest of the books and films going forward.

The Dark Knight

This movie has an interesting portrayal of Batman and the Joker that is somewhat grounded. I find the joker interesting here. He seems to revel too much in regards to what he does, but that makes him a great villain.

Jurassic Park

The special effects of this movie are wonderful for its time and is still felt alive in the modern day. This got me into dinosaurs as a young child.
